Problem Statement
Challenge
Traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) rely heavily on keyword matching, causing recruiters to overlook highly capable candidates whose skills and experience are expressed differently.

Pain Points
Keyword-based filtering

Hidden talent is missed

No semantic understanding

No reasoning behind rankings

Difficult to evaluate thousands of resumes quickly

Recruiters spend excessive time screening candidates

Goal
Build an AI system that understands both job descriptions and candidate profiles, reasons about their compatibility, and produces accurate, explainable rankings.

Our Vision
Build an AI Hiring Brain instead of another ATS.
